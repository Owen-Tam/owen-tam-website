Workflow

Getting Started

To get a clear picture of what you need, I would like an initial brief on what you want on your web page. Having this part helps for us to communicate on the features or parts that you want to have on your web page.

Designing

In this section, we can discuss on how your website will be created, along with a brief wireframe and design for your website. This way, you can get a brief overview of what your website might look like.

Development

After designing the web page, the next step is to develop it. This is a longer process than any of the other ones, since it takes time to write code a tailor it to your needs. I take the visuals that were dicussed and make them into something peopel can interact with!

Launch

Before we launch the website, I will teach you in-depth on what you need to know about the page, and how to use it! You can easily update it on your own after it.When it's time to launch the website, I'll try to take care of everything. All you need to do is to sit back and watch as your new web page go online!
